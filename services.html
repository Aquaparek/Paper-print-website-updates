Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our printing services

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept most common file formats including PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, INDD, JPG, and PNG. For best results, we recommend high-resolution PDFs with embedded fonts and images.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround times vary depending on the project complexity and quantity. Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days from approval of proof. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, our in-house design team can create custom designs for all your printing needs. We can work from your ideas or create something completely new based on your requirements.

What paper options do you offer?

We offer a wide range of paper options including different weights, finishes, and recycled options. Our team can help you choose the best paper for your project.

Do you offer samples before full production?

Yes, we can provide samples for most projects. There may be a nominal fee for samples, which is typically credited towards your final order.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. Business cards typically start at 100, while other printed materials may have different minimums. Contact us for specific product minimums.
