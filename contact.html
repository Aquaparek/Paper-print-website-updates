Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I get a quote for my printing project?

+

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form above, calling our office at (555) 123-4567, or emailing us at info@paperprint.com. Please provide as much detail as possible about your project including quantities, dimensions, paper type (if known), and your timeline.

What is your typical turnaround time?

+

Our standard turnaround time is 5-7 business days from proof approval. Rush services are available for an additional fee, with turnaround times as quick as 24-48 hours depending on the project. Please contact us for specific turnaround times for your project.

Do you offer design services?

+

Yes, we offer professional design services for all our printed products. Our experienced design team can create custom designs based on your specifications or enhance existing designs for optimal print quality. Design services are quoted separately based on project complexity.

What file formats do you accept?

+

We accept most common file formats including PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, INDD, JPG, and PNG. For best results, we recommend high-resolution PDFs with fonts embedded and images at 300 dpi. Our prepress team can work with you to ensure your files meet our requirements.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us for specific shipping quotes and estimated delivery times for your location.
