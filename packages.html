Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our packages

Can I modify items within a standard package?

Yes, we can modify items within our standard packages to better meet your needs. Please contact us to discuss your specific requirements, and we'll be happy to accommodate your requests where possible.

What is the turnaround time for packages?

Standard turnaround time for our packages is 10-14 business days from approval of proofs. Rush services are available for an additional fee, with turnaround times as quick as 5-7 business days.

Do you offer design services for the printed materials in the packages?

Yes, our packages include basic design services. Our design team will work with you to create designs that align with your brand and meet your specifications. More complex design work may incur additional charges.

Can I see samples of the products before ordering a package?

Yes, we can provide samples of specific products included in our packages. There may be a nominal fee for samples, which is typically credited towards your final order.

What payment methods do you accept for packages?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and company checks. For packages, we typically require a 50% deposit at the time of order, with the remaining balance due upon approval of final proofs before printing.

Do you offer discounts for larger orders or repeat customers?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for larger orders and loyalty discounts for repeat customers. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation and the discounts available to you.
